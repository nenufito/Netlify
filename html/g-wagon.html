TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The Mercedes-Benz G-Class combines uncompromising off-road capability with modern high-performance engineering. Depending on the version, it is powered by a range of robust engines, including the iconic 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8, delivering impressive power and torque. Paired with an advanced automatic transmission and a permanent all-wheel-drive system, the G-Wagon offers confident performance on any terrain.

Its ladder-frame construction, three locking differentials, and advanced suspension system ensure exceptional durability, traction, and control. Whether on asphalt or extreme off-road conditions, the G-Class delivers a unique balance of strength, stability, and refinement.
