TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The Bugatti Chiron is powered by an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine, delivering an extraordinary 1,500 horsepower. This immense power is managed through a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission and a sophisticated all-wheel-drive system, ensuring relentless acceleration and exceptional stability.

Thanks to its advanced aerodynamics, active suspension, and cutting-edge thermal management, the Chiron accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in under 2.5 seconds and reaches electronically limited top speeds exceeding 420 km/h, placing it among the fastest production cars ever built.
