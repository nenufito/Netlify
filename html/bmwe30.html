TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The BMW E30 M3 is powered by the legendary 2.3-liter naturally aspirated inline-four S14 engine, developed directly from BMW’s motorsport program. Producing up to 215 horsepower in its most powerful road-going versions, it is paired with a 5-speed manual transmission and rear-wheel drive.

Engineered for homologation racing, the E30 M3 features a lightweight body, widened track, and a finely balanced chassis. Its precise steering, high-revving character, and mechanical purity deliver an engaging and focused driving experience that defines classic performance driving.
