TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The Audi R8 gasoline engine range is available with a single engine option: a naturally aspirated 5.2-liter V10 with dry sump lubrication and direct injection. It is available in two different power outputs, one with 570 hp and the other with 620 hp. The latter version corresponds to the Audi R8 V10 Performance quattro, the most powerful model in the R8 range.

In both cases, the engine can only be paired with a seven-speed S tronic dual-clutch gearbox. Quattro all-wheel drive is standard on all Audi R8 models and is one of the hallmarks of our supercar.
