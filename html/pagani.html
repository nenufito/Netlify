TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The Pagani Zonda is powered by a naturally aspirated AMG-developed V12 engine, available in various displacements ranging from 6.0 to 7.3 liters, depending on the version. Output reaches up to 760 horsepower, delivered exclusively to the rear wheels through a manual or automated manual transmission, emphasizing driver involvement and mechanical purity.

Built around an ultra-lightweight carbon-fiber monocoque, the Zonda combines extreme rigidity with low weight. Its suspension, aerodynamics, and chassis tuning are engineered to deliver razor-sharp response, high-speed stability, and a visceral driving experience that prioritizes emotion, sound, and connection over digital intervention.

ACABADOS

Inside, the cabin is entirely handcrafted, blending leather, aluminum, carbon fiber, and machined metal components. Each switch, dial, and control is milled from solid metal, reflecting Pagani’s dedication to tactile quality and timeless design. The result is an interior that feels more like a bespoke timepiece than a conventional car—exclusive, mechanical, and unforgettable.
