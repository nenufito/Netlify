TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is engineered with one clear objective: maximum performance on track. It is powered by a high-revving naturally aspirated flat-six engine, producing 525 horsepower and capable of revving beyond 9,000 rpm. Power is delivered to the rear wheels through a 7-speed PDK dual-clutch transmission, ensuring lightning-fast gear changes and optimal lap times.

Advanced aerodynamics play a central role in the GT3 RS’s performance, generating significant downforce through its active aero elements, wide bodywork, and large rear wing. Combined with a lightweight construction, race-derived suspension, and precision steering, the GT3 RS delivers exceptional grip, balance, and control under extreme driving conditions.
