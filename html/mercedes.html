TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

The Mercedes-Benz 190E 2.5-16 Evolution II was engineered as a pure motorsport homologation model, developed to dominate the DTM championship. It is powered by a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated inline-four engine, producing up to 235 horsepower, paired with a 5-speed manual transmission and rear-wheel drive.

Its performance-focused setup includes a lightweight construction, revised suspension geometry, and advanced aerodynamics. Designed for precision and balance rather than outright speed, the Evo II delivers razor-sharp handling, high-revving performance, and an authentic race-bred driving experience that remains exceptional even by modern standards.
